Colony Clash

You awaken as a lone queen in a patch of damp earth. The colony’s future rests in your mandibles. Mate with a wandering male to seed your brood, dig chambers to shelter your young, and raise a caste system that will carry your will across the battlefield. In Colony Clash, every brood hatched is a step toward dominance.

Tutorial (3-Step Quickstart)

  1. Queen & Mate: Right-click to move the Queen. Find a male alate to unlock brood production.
  2. Dig & Produce: Open the Pheromone Wheel (Hold Space / Middle Click) to dig a chamber and queue a Nanitic.
  3. Raid & Defend: Drag-select multiple ants. Use the Pheromone Wheel to "Attack Move" into neutral resource nodes!
